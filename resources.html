Getting Started

How to Use These Resources

1

Watch the Teaching

Start with a StayTheWay video teaching on the topic that interests you. Find them on our Teachings page or YouTube channel.

2

Download the Handout

Grab the complementary study guide or handout that corresponds to the teaching you watched.

3

Discuss with Your Group

Gather friends, family, or your home Bible study group. Use the discussion questions and materials to lead meaningful conversations.

4

Apply & Grow

Take the truths you've learned and apply them to your daily walk with God. Let Scripture transform your life.
