Statement of Faith

The Way. The Truth. The Life.

"Jesus said to him, 'I am the way, the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father except through Me.'"

— John 14:6 (NKJV)

This is the foundation of everything we do at StayTheWay. Jesus didn't say He would show us a way — He said He is the Way. Our beliefs aren't a denominational checklist. They're the anchor that holds everything else in place.

Before You Read This

StayTheWay is a 501(c)(3) service ministry — not a denomination, not a competing church. We exist to equip believers wherever they are. These beliefs are what ground our teaching. We're not here to argue doctrine with your home church. We're here to point you to the Word and help you think biblically so you can test everything for yourself.

Like the Bereans in Acts 17:11, we encourage you to search the Scriptures daily to see if what we teach holds up. That's the whole point — learn how to think, not what to think.

What We Believe

Our Statement of Faith

The Word of God

We believe the Scriptures of the Old and New Testaments are the inspired, inerrant Word of God — the final authority on faith and practice. This isn't just a book of good ideas. It's the living, active Word that shapes how we live, how we think, and how we understand everything else. The Word of God supersedes any earthly law or cultural trend that contradicts it. It is the foundation this ministry is built on and the standard by which we measure every teaching.

Isaiah 28:13 · John 17:17 · 2 Timothy 3:16-17 · Hebrews 4:12 · 1 Peter 1:23-25 · 2 Peter 1:21

One God — Father, Son & Holy Spirit

We believe there is one living and true God, eternally existing in three persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit — equal in power and glory. This triune God created all things, upholds all things, and governs all things. He's not a distant force. He's a personal God who hears prayer, who intervenes in the affairs of men, and who saves from sin and death all who come to Him through Jesus Christ.

Genesis 1:1 · Deuteronomy 6:4 · Matthew 28:19 · John 10:30 · Hebrews 9:14

Jesus Christ — The Way, The Truth, The Life

We believe in the person of Jesus Christ — God's only begotten Son, conceived by the Holy Spirit. We believe in His virgin birth, His sinless life, His miracles and teachings, His substitutionary atoning death on the cross, His bodily resurrection, His ascension into heaven, His perpetual intercession for His people, and His personal, visible return to earth. Jesus is the only way to the Father. Period. No additions, no alternatives, no compromise.

Isaiah 7:14 · Matthew 1:23 · John 1:1-2, 8:58, 11:25, 14:6 · 1 Corinthians 15:3-4 · Hebrews 1:8 · Revelation 1:8

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the person of the Holy Spirit who came forth from the Father and Son to convict the world of sin, righteousness, and judgment — and to regenerate, sanctify, and empower for ministry all who believe in Christ. The Holy Spirit indwells every believer as an abiding helper, teacher, and guide. We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit and in the exercise of all the biblical gifts of the Spirit. We don't quench the Spirit, and we don't manufacture what isn't there. We stay yielded.

John 14:16-17, 16:8-11 · Acts 1:8 · Romans 12, 15:13 · 1 Corinthians 12, 14 · 2 Corinthians 3:18 · Hebrews 9:14

Sin & Salvation

We believe that all people are sinners by nature and therefore under condemnation. But God — rich in mercy — regenerates by the Holy Spirit those who repent of their sins and confess Jesus Christ as Lord. Salvation is by grace through faith. You can't earn it. You can't lose it by accident. And you certainly can't buy it. It's a gift. The most important decision any person will ever make is what they do with Jesus.

John 3:16 · Acts 8:15-17 · Romans 3:23, 6:23, 10:9-10 · Ephesians 2:8-9 · Titus 3:5

The Church

We believe in the universal Church — the living, spiritual body of which Christ is the head and all who are born again are members. The Church is not a building, a denomination, or a brand. It's every believer, everywhere. That's exactly why StayTheWay exists as a service ministry: we don't compete with churches, we strengthen believers within them. The only true basis of Christian fellowship is His agape love — which is greater than any doctrinal difference that divides us.

1 Corinthians 12:12-13 · Ephesians 4:15-16

Baptism & The Lord's Supper

We believe that the Lord Jesus Christ committed two ordinances to the Church: full-immersion water baptism and the Lord's Supper. Baptism is the outward declaration of an inward transformation — it's your public statement that you belong to Jesus. The Lord's Supper is our ongoing remembrance of what He did for us on the cross. These aren't rituals for ritual's sake. They're acts of obedience and worship.

Matthew 28:19 · Mark 1:9 · Acts 2:38 · 1 Corinthians 11:23-26

The Laying on of Hands

We believe in the laying on of hands for the baptism of the Holy Spirit, the ordination of pastors, elders, and deacons, and for the receiving and perpetuity of the gifts of the Holy Spirit. This is a biblical practice — not a theatrical one. It's how the early church operated, and it's how the Spirit continues to move when the body of Christ comes together in faith.

Acts 6:6, 8:17 · 1 Timothy 4:14

The Second Coming of Jesus Christ

We believe in the Second Coming of Jesus Christ — His personal, visible return to earth and the establishment of His millennial kingdom. We believe in the resurrection of the body, the final judgment, the eternal blessing of the righteous, and the endless separation of the wicked. We believe in the pre-tribulation rapture of the Church, where all believers will meet the Lord in the air and be taken out of this world prior to the tribulation. He's coming back. Stay ready.

Matthew 16:27, 24:29-31 · Acts 1:11 · 1 Thessalonians 5:9 · Revelation 19:11-16, 20:11-15

Heaven & Hell

We believe in a literal Heaven and a literal Hell. All those who place their faith, hope, and trust in Jesus Christ will spend eternity in Heaven with the Lord. Those who reject His free gift of salvation will spend eternity separated from God. We don't soften this. We don't water it down. The stakes are real, the love is real, and the invitation is open to everyone.

Matthew 5:3, 5:22, 18:9, 25:31-34 · Luke 12:5 · Hebrews 12:23 · 1 Peter 1:4

Marriage & Creation

We believe that God created man — male and female — different by design so as to complement and complete each other. Marriage has been ordained by God as the exclusive covenantal union of one man and one woman for a lifetime. God instituted this as the foundation of the family and the basic structure of human society. We hold to what Scripture teaches on this without apology, and with love for all people.

Genesis 2:24 · 1 Corinthians 6:9-11, 6:18-20, 7:1-3 · 1 Timothy 1:9-11

Worship

We believe worship of God should be spiritual — flexible and yielded to the leading of the Holy Spirit. We believe worship should be intelligent — rooted in the teaching of His Word so we know how God wants to be worshiped, not just how we feel like worshiping. And we believe worship should be fruitful — His love showing up in our lives as the supreme evidence that we've truly been in His presence. Worship is not a Sunday event. It's a way of life.

John 4:23-24 · Romans 12:1-2 · Colossians 3:16

Our Foundation

Love Over Division

We are not a denominational ministry, and we are not opposed to denominations — only to the over-emphasis of doctrinal differences that divide the Body of Christ. The only true basis of Christian fellowship is His agape love, which is greater than any difference we possess. Without that love, we have no right to call ourselves Christians.

This is why StayTheWay serves every church. We're not building walls — we're building bridges. If you love Jesus, hold to His Word, and want to grow deeper, we're here for you. Denomination doesn't matter. Labels don't matter. The Word matters. The Way matters.

Ready to Go Deeper?

These beliefs aren't walls — they're the foundation. Everything we teach, every resource we create, and every bridge we build stands on this ground. Now go study for yourself.
