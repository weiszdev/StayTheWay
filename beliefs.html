Statement of Faith

The Way. The Truth. The Life.

These aren't a denominational checklist. They're the anchor that holds everything else in place.

"Jesus said to him, 'I am the way, the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father except through Me.'"
John 14:6 (NKJV)

Jesus didn't say He would show us a way — He said He is the Way. This is the foundation of everything we do at StayTheWay.

Before You Read This

StayTheWay is a 501(c)(3) service ministry — not a denomination, not a competing church. We exist to equip believers wherever they are. These beliefs are what ground our teaching.

The Word of God

We believe the Scriptures of the Old and New Testaments are the inspired, inerrant Word of God — the final authority on faith and practice.

Scripture References

  • Isaiah 28:13
  • John 17:17
  • 2 Timothy 3:16-17
  • Hebrews 4:12
  • 1 Peter 1:23-25
  • 2 Peter 1:21

One God — Father, Son & Holy Spirit

We believe there is one living and true God, eternally existing in three persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit — equal in power and glory.

Scripture References

  • Genesis 1:1
  • Deuteronomy 6:4
  • Matthew 28:19
  • John 10:30
  • Hebrews 9:14

Jesus Christ — The Way, The Truth, The Life

We believe in the person of Jesus Christ — God's only begotten Son, conceived by the Holy Spirit. We believe in His virgin birth, His sinless life, His miracles and teachings, His substitutionary atoning death on the cross, His bodily resurrection, His ascension into heaven, His perpetual intercession for His people, and His personal, visible return to earth. Jesus is the only way to the Father. Period.

Scripture References

  • Isaiah 7:14
  • Matthew 1:23
  • John 1:1-2
  • John 8:58
  • John 11:25
  • John 14:6
  • 1 Corinthians 15:3-4
  • Hebrews 1:8
  • Revelation 1:8

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the person of the Holy Spirit who came forth from the Father and Son to convict the world of sin, righteousness, and judgment — and to regenerate, sanctify, and empower for ministry all who believe in Christ. The Holy Spirit indwells every believer as an abiding helper, teacher, and guide. We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit and in the exercise of all the biblical gifts of the Spirit.

Scripture References

  • John 14:16-17
  • John 16:8-11
  • Acts 1:8
  • Romans 12
  • Romans 15:13
  • 1 Corinthians 12
  • 1 Corinthians 14
  • 2 Corinthians 3:18
  • Hebrews 9:14

Sin & Salvation

We believe that all people are sinners by nature and therefore under condemnation. But God — rich in mercy — regenerates by the Holy Spirit those who repent of their sins and confess Jesus Christ as Lord. Salvation is by grace through faith.

Scripture References

  • John 3:16
  • Acts 8:15-17
  • Romans 3:23
  • Romans 6:23
  • Romans 10:9-10
  • Ephesians 2:8-9
  • Titus 3:5

The Church

We believe in the universal Church — the living, spiritual body of which Christ is the head and all who are born again are members. The Church is not a building, a denomination, or a brand. It's every believer, everywhere.

Scripture References

  • 1 Corinthians 12:12-13
  • Ephesians 4:15-16

Baptism & The Lord's Supper

We believe that the Lord Jesus Christ committed two ordinances to the Church: full-immersion water baptism and the Lord's Supper. Baptism is the outward declaration of an inward transformation. The Lord's Supper is our ongoing remembrance.

Scripture References

  • Matthew 28:19
  • Mark 1:9
  • Acts 2:38
  • 1 Corinthians 11:23-26

The Laying on of Hands

We believe in the laying on of hands for the baptism of the Holy Spirit, the ordination of pastors, elders, and deacons, and for the receiving and perpetuity of the gifts of the Holy Spirit.

Scripture References

  • Acts 6:6
  • Acts 8:17
  • 1 Timothy 4:14

The Second Coming of Jesus Christ

We believe in the Second Coming of Jesus Christ — His personal, visible return to earth and the establishment of His millennial kingdom. We believe in the pre-tribulation rapture of the Church. He's coming back. Stay ready.

Scripture References

  • Matthew 16:27
  • Matthew 24:29-31
  • Acts 1:11
  • 1 Thessalonians 5:9
  • Revelation 19:11-16
  • Revelation 20:11-15

Heaven & Hell

We believe in a literal Heaven and a literal Hell. All those who place their faith in Jesus Christ will spend eternity in Heaven. Those who reject His free gift will spend eternity separated from God.

Scripture References

  • Matthew 5:3
  • Matthew 5:22
  • Matthew 18:9
  • Matthew 25:31-34
  • Luke 12:5
  • Hebrews 12:23
  • 1 Peter 1:4

Marriage & Creation

We believe that God created man — male and female — different by design so as to complement and complete each other. Marriage has been ordained by God as the exclusive covenantal union of one man and one woman for a lifetime.

Scripture References

  • Genesis 2:24
  • 1 Corinthians 6:9-11
  • 1 Corinthians 6:18-20
  • 1 Corinthians 7:1-3
  • 1 Timothy 1:9-11

Worship

We believe worship of God should be spiritual, intelligent, and fruitful. Worship is not a Sunday event. It's a way of life.

John 4:23-24 · Romans 12:1-2 · Colossians 3:16

Love Over Division

We are not a denominational ministry, and we are not opposed to denominations — only to the over-emphasis of doctrinal differences that divide the Body of Christ. The only true basis of Christian fellowship is His agape love.
